About Us

What
we do

We are a team of specialists who know all the intricacies of laboratory analyses of environmental elements, biological studies of natural diversity, and expert assessment of compliance with the rules and regulations of living space.

More about us
Our advantages
  • You will get a high-quality result and save time, because we use modern equipment, constantly update technologies, and develop methods.
  • We cover environmental issues to the maximum extent possible, providing a wide range of research in various areas.
  • The experience of some of our experts exceeds 10 years! Be sure of the quality and punctuality of all research!
  • A large network of flora and fauna researchers, experts in assessing the ecology of living and industrial space.

Areas
of research

  • Air analysis

    The Institute includes an accredited testing laboratory, which has a modern laboratory base for conducting the widest range of air quality studies

  • Water analysis

    We provide services for the state registration of drinking water quality, namely, we conduct state tests for the purpose of biological and environmental assessment of pollutants
